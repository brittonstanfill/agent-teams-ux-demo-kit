How to read this prototype

Author: Visual Designer (v04-vd, revision pass for A11y + BS fixes) - working from the brief, CD anchor, UXR, IA, A11y, BS reports.

Three deliberate moves on first scan:

1. The operational header. Fact line, then state line. No icon, no hero image. Type is the hero, at 28px against deep ink on warm bone (#1A1715 on #F5F1E8).

2. Side-by-side path comparison, vertical stack. All three recovery paths visible at once with consequence copy as the design — never a tab pattern. Group-of-four party-size status is on the flight card itself, not behind a filter. Active selection is carried by a 2px teal border AND a 4px teal-ink left-edge bar — never by the teal-wash fill alone.

3. Persistent entitlements strip. Hotel and meal support live pinned to the bottom of screens 1-3, never inside an "Other policies" FAQ. One desaturated teal accent (#2E6E6B) is reserved for the active decision and nothing else. Sheet ships with a real focus trap (Esc, backdrop dismiss, inert underlying surface, focus returns to opener).

Screens stacked below (scroll through in order):

  1. SMS lock-screen card (the rewritten entry surface).
