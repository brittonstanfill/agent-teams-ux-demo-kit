# Creative Director — Director's Note

## 1. Aesthetic anchor

At 10:46 p.m. on a cancelled-flight screen, this product should feel like a calm night-shift dispatcher who already knows your name. Dark-mode-default, high-contrast neutrals — no brand-blue hero, no gradient. Type carries the weight: one large, plain-spoken headline (inferred: ~28–32px, regular or medium, not bold-shouted) stating the actual event in human words, with a quiet supporting line. Generous vertical rhythm — one decision per thumb-reach, not a stacked card carousel. Voice posture is direct, factual, slightly deferential: says what happened, what it can do, what it cannot, without marketing-cadence apology. Motion appetite near-zero — no skeleton-shimmer, no celebratory checkmarks, no progress-bar theater. The product's job at this hour is to lower the user's heart rate, not to perform competence.

## 2. Reference points

- **Things 3's confirmation register** — consequential actions stated plainly, consequence visible before the tap.
- **Apple Wallet boarding-pass density** — one screen carries only what you need, nothing competing.
- **Linear's empty/error states** — short sentences, structural confidence, no mascots, no exclamation marks.
- **iOS native share sheet rhythm** — options stack, primary path is obvious by weight not color, dismissal is one gesture.

## 3. The one quality bar

Every screen must be legible, decidable, and acted-on in under 15 seconds by a tired thumb in one hand — or it ships again. (Inferred; brief states no numeric target.)

## 4. Failure mode to avoid

A polite-airline apology page with a brand-blue hero, an illustrated suitcase, a "We're sorry for the inconvenience" subhead, and three equally-weighted option cards that force the user to comparison-shop their own disaster. Sympathy-theater over decision-support. This is the median output and we will not ship it.

## 5. Handoff to IA

- Which single decision must screen 1 force, and which decisions are deferrable to screen 2 without making the user feel they've committed before they understood?

## 6. Handoff to Visual Designer

- No hero illustration, no brand-color flood, no gradient. The cancellation event itself is the visual; typography and spacing carry the entire emotional register.
