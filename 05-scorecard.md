## What Counts As A Real Agent-Team Win

The team earns its cost when it does at least two of these:

- Finds issues the single agent missed.
- Produces a better flow because roles challenged each other.
- Catches accessibility or trust risks before synthesis.
- Makes disagreements visible and resolves them.
- Creates a better experiment plan than the baseline.

If the team only produces more text, it did not win.
