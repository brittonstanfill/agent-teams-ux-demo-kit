# Metacognition Checkpoint

Use this at the start and end of each improvement loop.

## Before

One-sentence hypothesis:

What does "better" mean for this run?

What would prove us wrong?

What are we likely to overvalue?

What must stay constant?

## During

Is the lead waiting for teammate work before synthesis?

Are teammates changing the artifact or only adding commentary?

Is the system preserving dissent?

Is the artifact getting better, or just more explained?

What blocker needs a nudge now?

## After

What surprised us?

Where did the simpler system win?

Where did coordination earn its cost?

What did we learn that generalizes?

What might be overfit to this scenario?

Next variable to hold constant:
